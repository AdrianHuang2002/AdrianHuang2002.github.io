I’m a first-year M.S. student in Computer Science at UMass Amherst, where I work with the Embodied AGI Group led by Chuang Gan. Previously, I was part of the UMD Gamma Group mentored by Xijun Wang and under the guidance of Dinesh Manocha. I’ve also collaborated with John P. Dickerson in the Dickerson Lab.

My current research interests are primarily centered on humanoid robots.
